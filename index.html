Al-Umrah

About Al-Umrah

(May God bless you, Umrah)

A sentence that the masses of Muslims from all over the world have always wished to repeat, and he said in its merits, may God’s prayers and peace be upon him: (Continue between Hajj and Umrah, for following between them removes poverty and sins as the bellows removes the impurity of iron). He, may God bless him and grant him peace, performed Umrah four times in his life, and performed one Hajj.

Al-Hajj

About Al-Hajj

- The Hajj is the fifth of the five pillars of Islam.

- Hajj was made obligatory in the ninth year of the Hijrah.

- Ihram for Hajj is from the spatial and temporal timings.

- Temporal times: They are the months specified to be a time for Hajj: Shawwal, Dhul-Qi’dah, and Dhul-Hijjah.
