About Manasek

Vision

The journey of Hajj, Umrah and visit should be codified, easy and easy in an atmosphere of tranquility and tranquility, to remain a distinctive and wonderful memory in the memory of the pilgrim, the Mu'tamar and the visitor to achieve his satisfaction, and make him an ambassador to convey to the world the Kingdom's efforts in serving the guests of the Rahman.

The Message

The Ministry of Hajj and Umrah works in the performance of its tasks in coordination with government and private entities to facilitate the procedures of performing rituals, control and legalize the service through the development of systems, the employment of technology, raising the efficiency of workers in the service of the guests of the Rahman, and completing the infrastructure with the aim of providing religious hospitality with international standards.

Objectives

The Ministry of Hajj and Umrah seeks to achieve many goals, and to create all service facilities to serve the guests of the Rahman, take care of them and pay attention to them, and the most prominent objectives are:

  • Work to improve the services provided to pilgrims and Umrah pilgrims.
  • Develop the work of the raft profession, agency and semantics, and pilgrimage inside using the latest technical means.
  • Work to ensure that the Hajj is easy and accessible through the human and technical efforts provided.
  • Upgrading the skills of field service workers to ensure distinguished services for pilgrims and Umrah pilgrims, by adopting distinguished and effective training programs.
  • Coordinate with all relevant authorities to facilitate the performance of pilgrims, Umrah pilgrims and visitors to their rituals.
  • Work in accordance with the values and teachings of the tolerant Islamic faith to provide distinguished services.
  • Unite efforts to motivate the citizen to contribute, as much as he can, to the service of the guests of the Rahman, whether commissioned or volunteered.
